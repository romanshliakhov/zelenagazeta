ВИРОБНИЦТВО

  • EVERY YEAR THE COMPANY INVESTS IN UPGRADING AND EXPANDING PRODUCTION CAPACITIES

    The uncompromising quality of the Сompany’s products is primarily determined by water, which serves as its basis. For the production of beverages, New Products Group uses only artesian water from local springs, characterized by optimal mineral composition for daily consumption. The highest quality of the artesian water encouraged the Company to launch the bottling line of drinking water PRYRODNE DZERELO™.

    The modern equipment installed at the Company’s production facility allows us to produce the widest range of packaging and beverages that meet all world quality and safety standards. High-speed bottling lines are operating at the plant, which allows for enterprise operation modernization.
